Wiring Up a Kegboard
====================

.. _pin-connections:

Pin Connections
===============

The following table describes the pins available on the Arduino board, and their
purpose in the Kegboard firmware.

+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| Digital Pin  | Description                                              |
+==============+==========================================================+
| 2            | Flowmeter A input                                        |
+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| 3            | Flowmeter B input                                        |
+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| 4            | Relay A output                                           |
+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| 5            | Relay B output                                           |
+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| 6            | Buzzer output                                            |
+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| 7            | Thermo A (1-wire)                                        |
+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| 8            | Thermo B (1-wire)                                        |
+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| 9            | Alarm output                                             |
+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| 10           | unused                                                   |
+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| 11           | Test pulse (output)                                      |
+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| 12           | unused                                                   |
+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
| 13           | unused                                                   |
+--------------+----------------------------------------------------------+
